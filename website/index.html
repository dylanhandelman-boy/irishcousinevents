Professional Event Services

Our Story

Meet Caroline & Maggie

What started as a passion for bringing people together has grown into something truly special. We're Caroline and Maggie — cousins, collaborators, and your personal event dream team in the Hudson Valley.

For over two years, we've been helping families and friends host unforgettable events without the stress. We treat every gathering like it's our own — because that's what family does.

From intimate backyard dinners to large milestone celebrations, we bring warmth, professionalism, and genuine care to every event we touch. Our roots are in this community, and we're proud to be a part of what makes it special.
